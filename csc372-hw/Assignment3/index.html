UNCG Dining

Make a Meal Plan

As college students, we've all been in that situation of just getting out of class and wondering where you should go to stop and get a bite to eat before your next class. Well, look no further! UNCG Dining will help you find just what you are looking for. Whether you are looking for a nice place to sit down and get a meal with friends or just grabbing a quick bite to eat before your next class, we have you covered. Here you will be able to find places to eat within walking distance and the places that suit your dietary needs. So, have a look at what we have to offer. I know you will find just what you are looking for.

Buffalo Wild Wings

Buffalo Wild Wings is located in the Elliot University Center (EUC) food court. Their specialties include wings, burgers, and wraps! They are open from 10:30am to 6:00pm every Monday-Thursday and 10:30am to 3:00pm on Friday. And they are also closed on the weekends.

Bojangles

Bojangles is located at 1501 Spring Garden St right across the street from the School of Education building. Their specialties include chicken tenders, breakfast biscuits, and sweet tea! They are open from 5:30am to 10:00pm every day of the week except on Sunday. On Sunday, they are open from 6:00am to 10:00pm.

Chick-fil-a

Chick-fil-a is also located in the EUC. However, it is located on the first floor right under the stairs. Their specialties include chicken sandwiches, waffle fries, and milkshakes! They are open from 7:30am to 10:00pm every Monday-Thursday, 7:30am to 5:00pm on Friday, 10:30am to 6:00pm on Saturdays, and of course they are closed on Sundays.
